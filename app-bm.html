BuddyMatch uses interactive questionnaires and artificial intelligence to match you with Platonic friends based on your Dislikes & Likes, then suggests activities to do together

Get Started Learn more

01

Answer a few Questions

We use a fun and interactive questionnaire so you don't get bored of answering questions. You can answer as many or as few questions as you'd like. Our designers are always trying to figure out ways to make this part a funner experience.

02

See your matches

Our Artificial intelligence algorithms get right to work and figure out the best matches for you, a match grade, and the probability of being good friends based on the number of questions you answer. Then we suggest activities for you and your new friend to do together.
